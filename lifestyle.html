Cat Health Tips:

  • Let your cat “hunt” for their food using puzzle feeders—great for mental stimulation and weight control


  • Turn grooming into a spa day with gentle brushing sessions and treat rewards to keep their coat healthy and shiny.


  • Set up a “cat gym” with climbing shelves or cardboard castles to keep them active and agile.


  • Play short games of chase-the-string or laser tag daily to burn energy and prevent boredom-related mischief.

    Cat Care Tips

     
  • Provide clean, fresh water daily and consider using a water fountain to encourage hydration.


  • Scoop the litter box at least once a day and clean it thoroughly once a week to prevent odor and stress.


  • Schedule regular vet checkups, even if your cat appears healthy; early detection of issues is key.


  • Enrich their environment with climbing spaces, scratching posts, and toys to prevent boredom and behavioral problems.


  • Brush regularly to reduce shedding and hairballs, especially in long-haired cats.
